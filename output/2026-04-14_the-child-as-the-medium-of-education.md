### Kernthese
Niklas Luhmann argues against teleological and psychological explanations of education, proposing instead the distinction between medium and form. He identifies the child as the "medium" of education, through which educational forms (specific communications, instructions) are realized, thereby providing a systems-theoretical account that can also explain the frequent failure of educational efforts.
