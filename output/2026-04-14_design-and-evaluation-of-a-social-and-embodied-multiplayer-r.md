## Design and evaluation of a social and embodied multiplayer reading game to engage primary school learners in Namibia

### Kernthese
The article presents the design and evaluation of "Spin Da Bottle," a social and embodied multiplayer reading game co-designed with primary school learners in Namibia. It argues that such a collective, embodied digital game can increase engagement in reading, scaffold weaker readers through peer support, and align with Ubuntu pedagogy for inclusive education.
