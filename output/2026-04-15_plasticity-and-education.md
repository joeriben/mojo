### Kernthese
This special issue introduction explores the relevance of Catherine Malabou's philosophy of plasticity for educational philosophy. It frames plasticity as a concept for understanding transformation, change, and the constitution of subjectivity, arguing that Malabou's work offers new resources for thinking about education beyond traditional humanist frameworks.
