### Kernthese
Gert Biesta reflects on his intellectual journey to “take education itself seriously,” critiquing the “learnification” of education (the reduction of education to learning discourse) and the global measurement industry that distorts educational practice. He presents his tripartite framework of educational purposes—qualification, socialization, and subjectification—as a way to recenter educational questions on content, purpose, and relationships, rather than measurable learning outcomes.
