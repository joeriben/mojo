### Kernthese
The article investigates whether an Instagram-based learning community can be inclusive regarding gender, ethnicity, and academic program. Using network analysis of posts and qualitative interviews, it finds that participants' centrality and influence in the community correlate with how they express their identities through photo sharing and discussion of environmental psychology concepts, suggesting identity expression fosters inclusiveness.
