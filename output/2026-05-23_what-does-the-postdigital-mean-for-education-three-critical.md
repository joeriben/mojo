### Kernthese
The article critically examines the concept of 'the postdigital' in education, rejecting a simple chronological view ('after the digital') in favor of a critical, sociomaterial perspective. It outlines three key fields of critique: the economic platformization of education, the metrification of institutional quality in educational policy, and the material infrastructures of the digital, including labor exploitation and resource extraction.
