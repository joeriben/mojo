### Kernthese
The article examines how Chinese TikTok account nurturers adapt their physical bodies to platform algorithms, developing an "algorithmic clock," precise movement patterns, and "suffering capital." It conceptualizes this as the "algorithmicized body," showing how platform algorithms penetrate and reshape users' physiological foundations and perceptual systems, extending disciplinary theory to algorithmic environments.
