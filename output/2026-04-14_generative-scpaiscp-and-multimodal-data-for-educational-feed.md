### Kernthese
The article empirically investigates the role of generative AI in providing formative feedback in children's embodied math learning using a digital body-scale number line. It compares AI-generated feedback with human instructor feedback, finding that while task performance doesn't differ, AI feedback leads to lower cognitive load and different visual information processing strategies. The study argues for hybrid intelligence approaches integrating AI and human feedback to enhance learning.
