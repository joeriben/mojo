### Kernthese
Biesta revisits and clarifies his tripartite framework of educational purposes (qualification, socialization, subjectification), focusing particularly on subjectification as relating to freedom—not freedom to do what one wants, but the "grown-up" freedom to act in and with the world, emphasizing the student as subject rather than object of educational interventions.

### Bemerkenswert
- Biesta's qualification-socialization-subjectification framework remains one of the most influential in contemporary educational theory; this article represents his continued refinement of it.
