### Kernthese
Hans-Christoph Koller presents a concept of transformational processes of Bildung, attempting to redefine the German notion with reference to actual social conditions of educational processes. Bildung is understood as a transformation of world- and self-relations that occurs when subjects confront problems for which they have no established routines, drawing on theories of Bourdieu, Waldenfels, and Oevermann to address how to grasp these relations, what triggers such processes, and how new figures of world- and self-relations emerge.
