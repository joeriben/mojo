### Kernthese
The article investigates Chinese EFL teachers' digital bridging initiatives that connect students' in-class and out-of-class language learning using digital resources. It identifies two dimensions (inward and outward bridging) and uses SEM to reveal determinants: teachers' awareness of students' out-of-class digital behaviors, school culture, educator-oriented identity belief, and TPACK.
